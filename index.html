BUILDING A PERSONAL COMPUTER

buidling your own personal computer is a dream, so i hope this webpage can help you processing and building your own personal computer

FOLLOW ME ON


1st STEP

you need to have all the parts needed and be sure to ready, if you wanted to have a good quality personal computer, make sure that the parts that you bought is brandnew and sealed, better quality better performance

2nd STEP

you have to carefully insert all the parts one by one and make sure that it's screwed not so tight but not so loose either

3rd STEP

after inserting all the parts, it's time to plug in each of every parts cables to the pin in the motherboard, like SATA, CPU COOLER/HEATSINK, and most importantly 24PIN for the power

4th STEP

this may be the most annoying step but massively important, cable management, after plugging all the pins make sure to manage the cables to prevent overheating and because it affects the airflows

5th STEP

after finishinng the unit now it's time to buy and connect the peripherals, table and chair is optional but it's best to have a new

6th STEP

after connecting the peripherals, it's time to download your operating system, the best one for now is windows 10 (my own opinion) but the latest is 11 after installing the operating system your pc is good to go
